Why Source of Truth Matters

Clean data is the foundation. Without it, nothing else works.

The Problem Today

  • Multiple systems with different data
  • Duplicate contacts and companies
  • SDRs waste time on stale leads
  • Manual account allocation

What Clean Data Enables

  • Automated lead routing
  • Reliable scoring and prioritisation
  • AI that can actually help
  • SDRs focused on conversations

You can't automate or use AI on data you can't trust.

Are these duplicates? Which should merge?

Simple Rules

"Domain matches → merge"

Merges A + B (wrong — different subsidiaries)

Keeps C separate (wrong — same org)

Breaks data, loses deals

Risk Thresholds & Human Review

Not every match needs a human. But risky ones do.

High Confidence
⚡ Auto-merge
Medium Confidence

Low Confidence

Speed where it's safe

Obvious duplicates merge automatically — no bottleneck on easy cases

🛡️

Safety where it matters

Tricky cases get human judgment — no bad merges breaking data

The threshold is tuneable. Start cautious (more human review), then raise automation as confidence grows.
